### Ad settings

Executing Your First X & Reddit Ad Setup Safely

Since you are funding this out of your own pocket, you must use strict guardrails to ensure you don’t waste money on empty clicks:

#### The Reddit Ad Settings:

Objective: Choose Traffic or Conversions. Never select "Reach."
Targeting: Do not target general interests. Use Community Targeting and manually type in your exact target subreddits (e.g., r/golang, r/GrowthHacking). This ensures your ad is only seen by your precise target personas.

Device: Restrict to Mobile Devices Only. People doom-scrolling on their phones will hit your clean Cloudflare landing page and opt-in in two seconds.

#### The X Ad Settings:

Targeting: Use Follower Lookalikes. 

Tell X to show your ad only to followers of deep-tech engineering leaders, elite technical VCs, or corporate developer tooling companies (like Vercel, Supabase, or Stripe Devs). You are catching those specific users exactly while they are trapped in the timeline loop.

3. Combining Follower Look-Alikes with the "Doom-Scroll Hook"

Because you are targeting people who actively track these top-tier handles, your creative copy needs to acknowledge the precise context of why they are seeing your message.

Since they follow elite dev tools, they are sophisticated users who value clear engineering text and hate low-effort updates. Your ad layout should leverage this directly:

You follow elite tech channels. But the algorithm wants you scrolling. 🧵Accounts like @Vercel and @supabase drop masterclass-level long-form posts on this timeline. But you have to wade through dozens of memes, political debates, and random trends just to find one.

To prevent X from burning through your budget on broad traffic, enforce these strict administrative toggles inside your Ad Group settings:

Uncheck "Optimized Targeting": X has a checkbox that says "Allow optimized targeting to expand reach outside your parameters". Turn this off immediately. If left checked, X will ignore your specific follower lookalike lists and display your ad to general consumer profiles to spend your daily budget faster.

Device Filtering: Force the system to serve your ad only to Mobile Devices (iOS and Android). Users browsing X on their phones are in the flow-state of scrolling and will complete your simple landing page email input loop far faster than desktop users
