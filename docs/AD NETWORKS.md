# Ad Buyer Networks

1. Passionfroot (The Best for Independent Creators)

Passionfroot is specifically built for creators who want to stay independent but hate the back-and-forth of selling ads.

How it works: They provide you with a beautiful, professional, and branded "Storefront/Media Kit" page that you link to from your Feedfree footer.

The Workflow: Advertisers browse the Passionfroot directory, see your metrics (subscriber count, open rates, categories), select open dates on your calendar, upload their ad copy, and pay via credit card. You just click "Approve" or "Reject.

"The Fee: They don't charge a high monthly fee; instead, they take a small percentage commission on successful ad transactions.

2. Paved (The B2B Marketplace Giant)

Paved is a massive marketplace specifically designed to connect newsletters with corporate advertisers. It is a favorite for tech, coding, and marketing publications.

How it works: You create a profile in their marketplace. Corporate brands (like HubSpot, Monday.com, or digital infrastructure tools) search Paved for specific tags like "Software Engineering" or "SaaS Marketing."

The Advantage: Paved has an automated feature where they can instantly track your list metrics, giving premium advertisers deep confidence in your data. They also handle the automated billing and payouts cleanly.

3. Swapstack (Great for Early-Stage Traction)

Swapstack is another excellent independent ad marketplace that caters heavily to small-to-mid-sized newsletters.

How it works: They feature an active, inbound marketplace. Instead of just waiting for brands to find Feedfree, you can browse a list of active campaigns from brands looking to sponsor tech/business newsletters and click "Apply" to pitch them your slot.

4. BuySellAds (The Dev & Tech Standard)

BuySellAds is a premium marketplace where corporations specifically go to buy developer, designer, and tech inventory.

How you use it: You create a profile detailing your metrics (subscribers, open rates, categories) and set your own flat prices.

The Control Level: Advertisers find you via their directory and submit a request. You get an alert showing exactly what their ad text says. You can click "Accept" or "Reject". If you accept, they pay BuySellAds, you copy-paste the text into your open-source template, and BuySellAds takes a cut of the payout.
