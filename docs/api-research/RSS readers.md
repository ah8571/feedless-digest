### Reddit subreddits

"Open RSS feeds are available for many different areas on Reddit and offers unique benefits when compared to Reddit's feeds.

Full content in all feeds
Includes images
Supports old.reddit.com links
Individual feeds for comments made in a Reddit post
Feeds filtered by a Reddit search term
## Subreddit feed

Navigate to any subreddit on reddit.com (e.g. reddit.com/r/modhelp).
Optionally apply any filters by sorting by Rising, Hot or Top (defaults to New)
Optionally apply frequency by sort by dropdown to Now, Today, This Week This Month, This Year or All Time.
While on the page, add openrss.org/ at the beginning of
the URL of the page in your browser bar.
Navigate to the updated URL to generate the feed.
Add the feed to your reader app." - openrss
